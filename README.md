# Data, Computation & Innovation II

Columbia Graduate School of Journalism · Summer 2026  
June 3 through July 15 · Wednesday 2:00–5:00 · Pulitzer 501A

**Instructor:** Jared Whalen · [jmw2320@columbia.edu](mailto:jmw2320@columbia.edu)  
**Office hours:** [Schedule virtual office hours](https://calendar.app.google/pWbmdwuNDLKBYmeA7) (24 hours notice). For in-person meetings, email to arrange a time.
